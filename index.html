How are finals calculated?

Your four terms grades are added together, and then that sum is doubled. The grade you get on the final is added to that sum, and the new total is divided by 9 to calculate your overall final grade.

Basically: ((2 * [term 1 + term 2 + term 3 + term 4]) + final) / 9 = overall grade
